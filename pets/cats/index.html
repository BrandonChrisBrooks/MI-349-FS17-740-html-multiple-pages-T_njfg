Cats

Woah there! A little stressed are we? Maybe you should get a cat. Yes that's right, I said get a cat! The sounds of a cat's purr can lower your blood pressure. Oh but you want someone who can play fetch? Cats can do it too! They are very easy to housebreak so you don't have to spend weeks potty training another mammal. You can bring your cat with you!!! Going on a trip? Grab the cat and the cat carrier and be on your way. You don't have to put your pet for a million (billion) dollars for someone who is going to put your cat in a cage anyways. Do it yourself, have the comfort of your cat, and save money! Cats are the best!
